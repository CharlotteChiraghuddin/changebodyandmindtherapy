Sessions & fees

Session information

I offer both online and in-person therapy sessions for adults aged 18 and over. Sessions are available on both a short-term and long-term basis, depending on your individual needs and what feels right for you.

Session details

Each therapy session lasts for 50 minutes and takes place in a safe, supportive and confidential environment.

Availability

Monday to Friday · 9:00am – 9:00pm
Evening appointments are available.

Location

Online via Google Meet, or in-person in Harlow, Essex.

Fees

Individual therapy session (50 minutes)
£55 per session · Online or in person.

Concession rate · £45 per session

Concession rates are available for:

  • Key workers
  • Those currently receiving benefits
  • Refugees
  • Older adults / OAPs

Introductory phone call

A free 15-minute introductory phone call can be offered prior to booking, giving you the opportunity to ask any questions and see whether therapy with me feels right for you.

Arrange a free call

Payment & cancellation policy

Payment for sessions is required at least 48 hours in advance in order to secure your appointment time.

If you need to cancel or rearrange a session, I kindly ask for at least 48 hours notice. Sessions cancelled with less than 48 hours notice may still be charged in full.

This policy is in place to protect both your appointment space and the availability of sessions for other clients.

Taking the first step into therapy can sometimes feel daunting, but you are welcome to contact me if you would like to ask any questions or arrange an initial session.

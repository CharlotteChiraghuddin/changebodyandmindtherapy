title: An experience of reading a book and opening my eyes.
date: 2026-07-08T11:52:26.592Z

"You will never succeed"

"You failed"

"Your stupid"

"See? Told you, just give up"



Are only a few thoughts I have had about myself.



I listened to them and believed them. I quit a lot of things, or didn't even try things because of this voice. It's something I never understood, but it really frustrated and upset me.



Even through therapy, I never understood it. We spoke about how I spoke to myself, but there was no explanation and I didn't know how to stop it.



Until I found a book as I was doing some CPD and read a book called "the imp of the mind" by Lee Baer. The book is about intrusive thoughts, but I found an answer within the pages. The imp is that voice. It pushes the bad thoughts through.....
